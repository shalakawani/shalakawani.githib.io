2 Pizzas, 67 Songs, and a Weekend

The world of Generative AI ignited my imagination in class. Watching someone craft a product ad in minutes made me wonder – could I build my own website? The challenge: I had zero coding experience, a weekend packed with exams, and a mountain of assignments. But, I was determined to try. I spent the entire weekend glued to my chair. I treated this like a high-stakes project, breaking it down into manageable pieces. First, I had to learn the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. ChatGPT became my indispensable AI tutor, explaining concepts, debugging my code, and even offering design tips. I set mini-deadlines, constantly iterating and improving. There were frustrating moments when code wouldn't work or designs looked wonky. But seeing my ideas take shape kept me going. After 2 pizzas, multiple loops of playlist of 67 songs, a lot of web surfing, sitting on a chair the entire weekend, this website was born. It might not be flawless, but it's proof of what you can achieve with AI as your guide and a project management mindset focused on the finish line. Explore my journey so far below...
